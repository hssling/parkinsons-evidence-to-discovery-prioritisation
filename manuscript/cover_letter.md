Please consider our manuscript, "Artificial intelligence-assisted evidence mapping and computational prioritisation of preventive and disease-modifying strategies for Parkinson's disease: a systematic evidence-to-discovery study," for publication in Journal of Neural Transmission.

The manuscript fits the journal's translational neuroscience and neurology scope by integrating evidence mapping, clinical-trial mining, public transcriptomics, GO/Reactome pathway enrichment, pathway-to-intervention network analysis, drug-repurposing prioritisation, and public-health translation.
