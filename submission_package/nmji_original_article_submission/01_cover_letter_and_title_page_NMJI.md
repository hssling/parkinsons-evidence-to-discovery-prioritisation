## Cover Letter

Date: 26 April 2026

To  
The Editor  
*The National Medical Journal of India*

Re: Submission of an Original Article

Please consider our manuscript, **"AI-assisted evidence mapping and prioritisation of preventive and disease-modifying strategies for Parkinson disease: an evidence-to-discovery study,"** for publication as an **Original Article** in *The National Medical Journal of India*.

This study integrates evidence mapping, clinical-trial mining, public transcriptomic analysis, pathway enrichment, and translational prioritisation to identify practical and research-oriented intervention points for Parkinson disease. Its clinical and public-health relevance to India lies in the emphasis on pesticide safety, physical activity, metabolic health, air-pollution exposure, head-injury prevention, and realistic prioritisation of investigational disease-modifying strategies.

The manuscript has been prepared in line with NMJI's original article format, including a structured abstract, IMRAD organisation, anonymised main manuscript, and a maximum of five main tables/figures. Additional display items have been moved to supplementary material.

The author confirms that this manuscript is original, has not been published previously in full, and is not under consideration elsewhere. Any prior abstract, conference presentation, repository posting, or related public release should be disclosed in the submission system or in subsequent communication with the journal if applicable.

The author further confirms that the manuscript has been reviewed and approved for submission, that authorship satisfies ICMJE criteria, that conflicts of interest and funding have been declared, and that the use of artificial intelligence-assisted tools in the research and manuscript workflow has been disclosed in line with NMJI, ICMJE, and WAME guidance.
