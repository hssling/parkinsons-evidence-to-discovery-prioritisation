Release v1.0.1 was created after GitHub-Zenodo connection to trigger Zenodo archiving; the Zenodo DOI should be inserted here after Zenodo processing is complete. Public source data are available from the cited GEO accessions, ClinicalTrials.gov records, and official database URLs listed in the audit files. Reactome, GO, benchmark, and recurrence analyses are reproducible from the saved scripts and package audit files. The computational outputs are intended for research and hypothesis generation only and do not establish clinical efficacy, prevention, disease modification, or cure.
