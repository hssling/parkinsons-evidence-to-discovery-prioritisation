# Journal Selection

## Selected First-Choice Journal

**Journal of Neural Transmission** should be the first submission target if the goal is a Springer Nature journal with an APC-free route.

Rationale:

- It is a Springer Nature hybrid journal covering translational neuroscience, neurology, preclinical neurological studies, psychiatry, and preclinical psychiatric studies.
- It has a clear subscription publishing route.
- Springer states that authors can choose subscription publishing for this journal and that no APC charges apply under that route.
- It has published Parkinson's disease therapeutic and prevention-oriented review content, making the scope fit credible for this evidence-to-discovery manuscript.

Important submission instruction:

Choose **subscription publishing model / non-open-access route** after acceptance. Do **not** choose Open Access unless APC funding or a waiver is confirmed.

Official fee note checked April 2026:

- Journal of Neural Transmission is hybrid.
- Open-access APC listed: GBP 3190 / USD 4990 / EUR 3990.
- Subscription route: no APC charges apply.
- Source: https://link.springer.com/journal/702/how-to-publish-with-us

## Why Not Discover As First Choice

The Discover journals are not the best APC-free route. Springer states that authors publishing open access in Discover journals are required to pay an article-processing charge, although waivers or discounts can be requested. That makes Discover a waiver-dependent option, not a reliably APC-free option.

Source: https://www.springer.com/gp/campaign/discover-journals/fees-and-funding
